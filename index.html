Challenges & Conclusion Not everything went as planned. I’m sure not every project goes 100% as planned. GoalsI concluded that time, budget and the size of the group contributed to the stress of the booking process. The solution for the user might be to help the user by developing an app that will take their information and recommend potential vacation packages based on what the user (customer) can afford with a limited budget and time. Let the app do the “heavy lifting” for the next trip.
The Challenge or Problem StatementIt’s challenging and daunting planning a vacation nowadays with restrictions that can affect when and where you want to go. According to the conducted interviews, time, budget and size of the group were the major stress factors that affected their vacation planning. How might we take these known factors so that the app can simplify the entire process of booking a vacation and give the confidence and peace of mind back to the user?
ROLE/TEAMMy role in this project was that of a UX designer as this was an individual project.
TOOLSFigma, Miro, Interviews, Surverys, Personas, User Journey, Sketches, Wireframes, User Testing
CLIENT/DATE/DURATIONThe target audience was career driven people who had limited amount of vacation time and wanted to get the most out of their trip. The goal was to launch an app that would help booking a vacation less stressful by having options readily available in one location on the week of their stay.
Project overview: Imagine you’ve just been hired as a product designer for a new travel startup and the startup plans is to launch the app once the global Pandemic ends.
